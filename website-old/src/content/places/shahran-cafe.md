## About

Shahran Cafe is the kind of place you'd walk past a hundred times without noticing — and that's part of its charm. Tucked away in the bylanes of Old City, this unassuming cafe has been serving up authentic Irani chai for over four decades.

## What to Order

- **Irani Chai** — The star of the show. Sweet, milky, and perfectly brewed
- **Osmania Biscuits** — Crispy, sweet, and perfect for dipping
- **Bun Maskaa** — Soft bun slathered with butter

## The Vibe

With its vintage marble-top tables, slow-moving ceiling fans, and walls adorned with old photographs, Shahran Cafe feels like a step back in time. It's the perfect spot to escape the chaos of the city and watch the world go by.

## Practical Info

**Timings:** 6 AM - 10 PM  
**Budget:** ₹50-100 per person  
**Best Time:** Early morning for the freshest chai
