Welcome to **Lost in Hyd** — your weekly dose of everything Hyderabad has to offer beyond the obvious.

## What to Expect

Every week, we'll bring you:

- **Hidden Eats**: Unassuming joints that serve the best biryani, chai, or street food
- **Local Events**: Cultural happenings, gigs, and community meetups  
- **Offbeat Spots**: Temples, stepwells, and forgotten corners of the city
- **Neighborhood Stories**: Deep dives into areas like Moazzam Jahi Market, Shahran, or Gachibowli's hidden lanes

## Our Mission

Hyderabad is a city of contrasts — where centuries-old architecture stands beside gleaming tech campuses, where traditional markets thrive alongside modern cafes. There's so much to explore, but the best spots often stay hidden from casual visitors and even longtime residents.

That's where we come in.

## Coming Up Next Week
